## TESTING STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

### Phase 1: Validation Test (Week 1-2)

**Test Configuration:**
- **Pages to test:** vitamix-genz-v8, vitamix-genz-v6, vitamix-genz-v2 (top 3)
- **Testing method:** Multi-armed bandit algorithm (e.g., Google Optimize with automatic traffic allocation)
- **Success metric:** CTA click-through rate (primary: "Explore Sustainable Recipes" / "Get Recipe" clicks; secondary: scroll depth past 50%)
- **Minimum sample size:**
  - For 5% baseline CTR, 80% power, 95% confidence to detect 20% lift: ~3,850 visitors per variant = 11,550 total
  - For Gen Z mobile-heavy traffic, assume 60% mobile: ensure mobile load speed < 3 seconds
- **Statistical significance target:** 95% confidence
- **Test duration:** 7-14 days (depending on traffic volume)

**Rationale:**
Multi-armed bandit over traditional A/B test because:
1. **Faster learning**: MAB automatically shifts traffic to winning variants as confidence builds vs waiting for full sample size
2. **Lower opportunity cost**: With 3 variants, traditional A/B test would expose 33% of traffic to losers for entire test duration; MAB minimizes this
3. **Gen Z behavior volatility**: Younger audiences show higher variance in behavior; MAB adapts faster to emerging patterns

Start with top 3 (not top 5) because:
- v4 (community-first) is fundamentally different strategy, not optimization‚Äîtest separately if targeting warm traffic
- brandy scored too low (68/100) to justify test traffic‚Äî15 points below winner

## APPENDIX: Methodology

### Analysis Framework

**7 Scoring Dimensions (0-10 scale per dimension):**

1. **Audience Alignment (Weight: 20%)**
   - Does page speak directly to Gen Z needs, values, and preferences?
   - Language and tone match expectations (authentic vs forced)
   - Imagery reflects audience lifestyle and identity
   - Message addresses audience-specific pain points (sustainability, cost, time)
   - Cultural relevance and authenticity

2. **Device/Platform Optimization (Weight: 20%)**
   - Mobile responsiveness (Gen Z is 85% mobile)
   - Load speed on mobile devices
   - Touch-friendly interactions
   - Viewport optimization and scrolling behavior
   - Mobile-specific features (compressed images, simplified nav)

3. **Attention & Engagement (Weight: 15%)**
   - Time to value proposition (Gen Z 8-second attention span)
   - Cognitive load (simple vs complex information)
   - Visual hierarchy and scanability
   - Content format match (visual vs text-heavy)
   - Engagement hooks and scroll triggers

4. **Visual Design & Appeal (Weight: 15%)**
   - Design trends relevant to Gen Z preferences (modern, authentic, not corporate)
   - Color psychology appropriate for demographic
   - Image quality and diversity
   - Professional vs casual styling match
   - Brand perception alignment (innovative, sustainable, accessible)

5. **Value Communication (Weight: 15%)**
   - Clarity of main benefit/offer
   - Benefits vs features balance
   - Problem-solution alignment
   - Unique value proposition strength
   - Urgency and relevance

6. **Trust & Credibility (Weight: 10%)**
   - Social proof type and quality (peer reviews > brand claims)
   - Testimonial authenticity and relevance
   - Security/privacy indicators
   - Brand credibility signals
   - Third-party validation (ratings, certifications)

7. **Values & Purpose Alignment (Weight: 5%)**
   - Sustainability/environmental messaging (critical for Gen Z)
   - Social responsibility and ethical practices
   - Transparency and honesty
   - Purpose beyond profit
   - Community impact

**Overall Score Calculation:**
```
Overall Score =
  (Audience Alignment √ó 0.20) √ó 10 +
  (Device Optimization √ó 0.20) √ó 10 +
  (Attention & Engagement √ó 0.15) √ó 10 +
  (Visual Design √ó 0.15) √ó 10 +
  (Value Communication √ó 0.15) √ó 10 +
  (Trust & Credibility √ó 0.10) √ó 10 +
  (Values Alignment √ó 0.05) √ó 10
```

**Weight Adjustments Made:**
- **Increased Device Optimization to 20%** (from standard 15%) due to Gen Z mobile-first behavior
- **Maintained Values Alignment at 5%** despite Gen Z prioritizing sustainability because it's woven into Audience Alignment scoring‚Äîavoiding double-counting
- **Standard weights for other dimensions** appropriate for Gen Z CTA click-through goal
